ABOUT

Pieter-Jan Declercq is a young and creative photographer with an eye for detail. He started combining his passions for exclusive cars and photography at a young age and gradually developed his own, distinct style. While pursuing his career as a mechanical engineer, Pieter-Jan has gained experience in all sorts of photography, with most projects related to the automotive sector. In recent years, he has been working for private clients and companies all around Europe.
